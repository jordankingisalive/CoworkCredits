Copilot Cowork Credit Chargeback

Turn Entra + Copilot credit exports into a departmental chargeback report — entirely in your browser.

All processing happens locally in your browser. Your files never leave your device. No uploads, no servers, no telemetry. Everything you see is computed on this page from the files you choose.

How it works

1

Export your Entra users

From Microsoft Entra admin center, export the user list as CSV. It supplies the organizational attributes — department, business unit, job family, cost center, country, and manager.

Expected columns (any subset): User Principal Name, Display Name, Department, Job Title, Job Family, Cost Center, Business Unit, Country / Usage Location, Manager.

2

Export your Copilot credit usage

From the Copilot admin / Cost Management report, export monthly credit usage as CSV. It supplies usage — credits used, credit limit, license status, last activity, and session count.

Expected columns: User Principal Name, Monthly credits used, Monthly credit limit, Microsoft 365 Copilot license, Last activity date, Session Count.

3

Generate the report

The two files are joined on user principal name (UPN). We compute per-user overage, utilization, cohorts, and a headline chargeback based on individual overage. Slice by any dimension and adjust the rate live.
